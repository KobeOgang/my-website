My Games

Static Shift

Lead Programmer (Ongoing)

A narrative puzzle game where you wield telekinetic powers. Trapped in a mysterious and abandoned lab with no memory, you must find your way out using your abilities. All while discovering your past and the secrets of the lab.

KleinHein

Solo Developer (October 31)

A top-down arcade shooter multiplayer game, where you fight against other players. With a leaderboard system, the one with the most eliminations before the timer ends wins.

Math-Teroids

Solo Developer (July 4)

An educational arcade game where you defend Earth from incoming asteroids by solving the math problems they carry. Correct answers let you launch missiles to destroy them.

Fear and Memory

Lead Programmer (September 28)

An atmosphere-driven survival horror game designed as a reflective medium on the Philippine Martial Law era. Players take on the role of Dante, a journalist, as he navigates in a mysterious world, while recovering his fragmented memories.

Graviti

Solo Developer (March 15)

A 2D pixel puzzle-platformer where you can manipulate gravity to solve challenges. Push boxes, activate buttons, open doors, and use gravity to your advantage. All while avoiding spikes.

The Blacksmith

Programmer (March 9)
